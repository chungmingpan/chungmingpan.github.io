I am taking part in the SLMath program Special Geometric Structures and Analysis as a Postdoc SLMath during Fall 2024. Starting in January 2025, I will be a postdoc at Université du Québec à Montréal as part of the research group CIRGET.

I received my PhD from Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse, Université Toulouse III - Paul Sabatier in June 2023, under the supervision of Vincent Guedj and Henri Guenancia. For the academic year 2023-2024, I was an ATER (one-year postdoc with teaching duties) at IMT, UPS.

My research interests focus on Complex Geometry and Differential Geometry.
Some specific topics are: canonical and special metrics, complex Monge-Ampère equations, and pluripotential theory.
