I am an ATER (one-year postdoc with teaching duties) at Université Paul Sabatier, Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse for the academic year 2023-2024.

I am currently on the job market. Here is my CV (updated Jan. 03, 2024).

My research interests focus on Complex Geometry and Differential Geometry. Some specific topics are: canonical metrics, complex Monge-Ampère equations, and pluripotential theory.
