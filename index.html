I am a postdoc in the research group CIRGET, Université du Québec à Montréal, mentored by Vestislav Apostolov.

I received my PhD from Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse, Université Toulouse III - Paul Sabatier in June 2023, under the supervision of Vincent Guedj and Henri Guenancia.

For the academic year 2023-2024, I was an ATER (one-year postdoc with teaching duties) at IMT, UPS. During Fall 2024, I was taking part in the SLMath program Special Geometric Structures and Analysis as a Postdoc SLMath mentored by Song Sun.

My research interests focus on Complex Geometry and Differential Geometry.
Some specific topics are: canonical Kähler metrics, special hermitian metrics, complex Monge-Ampère equations, and pluripotential theory.
